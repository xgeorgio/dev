Java-based on-line system for protein prediction,
using Artificial Neural Networks.

Welcome!

to the first Java-based client/server application for protein prediction, using Artificial Neural Networks. PEGASUS system is a very valuable tool for analyzing amino acid sequences of DNA and recognizing protein patterns for further examination. It is an absolutely FREE service and is directed mainly to Molecular Biologists and Genetic Engineers.
